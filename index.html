Our Mission

Our Mission at Up Market is to give apparel bound for the landfill a new life. 92 million tons of clothes are thrown away globally each year, filling landfills, ending up in waterways, and harming the planet in which we live. We value ingenuity and resourcefulness, using textiles otherwise bound for the landfill, reprocessing them, and making new stylish looks from them.
